Platform

  1. Our core technology is the Mosaic™ - a dynamic, proprietary media format optimized for mobile viewing, sharing and seamless purchase.
  2. Our mobile platform, LENS™, allows businesses to easily aggregate, curate and publish their content - as Mosaics - directly to customers.
  3. LENS™ operates with geo-fenced networks. Once you physically enter a network, you are introduced to activities, offers, exclusive content, information and more within steps of where you are.

Our Work

Nearly every place in today’s world is paired with a digital counterpart. People interact with museums, neighborhoods, restaurants and more in real life, and virtually online via a multitude of devices. We are here to bridge the distance between those two places, with our LENS™ application. LENS™ creates place identity for locations where they can come alive with digital information, entertainment and commerce.
